Diagnosing Car Engine Problems: Common Issues, Symptoms, and Smart Solutions

Car engine problems can range from mild vibrations to sudden power loss. Recognizing the symptoms early — and understanding their likely causes — can help you avoid costly repairs. In this guide, we explore the most common engine issues and how modern tools like Carithm AI make diagnosis easier.

Common Symptoms of Engine Trouble

  • Loss of power or acceleration
  • Rough idling or shaking
  • Knocking or pinging noises
  • Misfires or inconsistent firing
  • Engine overheating
  • Check Engine Light (CEL) on

Top Engine Issues and Their Causes

1. Engine Misfire

Symptoms: Jerky acceleration, rough idle, decreased fuel efficiency

Causes: Spark plug failure, ignition coil issues, fuel injector problems

Fix: Use an OBD-II scanner or try Carithm AI to decode fault codes like P0300 and get suggestions.

2. Engine Knocking

Symptoms: Metallic rattling or tapping under load

Causes: Low-octane fuel, timing problems, carbon buildup

Fix: Try higher-octane fuel first. Persistent knocking could indicate a serious timing or detonation issue.

3. Overheating

Symptoms: Steam, high-temperature gauge, coolant leaks

Causes: Low coolant, radiator issues, thermostat failure

Fix: Refill coolant and check for leaks. If the problem persists, pressure test the system.

4. Rough Idle

Symptoms: Shaky engine when stopped, fluctuating RPM

Causes: Vacuum leaks, dirty throttle body, worn motor mounts

Fix: Clean throttle body, inspect idle air control valve (IAC), and check for air leaks.

5. Hard Start or No-Start

Symptoms: Cranks but doesn’t start, long crank times

Causes: Weak battery, crankshaft sensor failure, clogged fuel system

Fix: Start with a battery and fuel pressure test. Use Carithm AI to help isolate the issue with code-based diagnostics.

Preventative Engine Maintenance Tips

  • Change spark plugs every 30,000–60,000 km
  • Check and top coolant regularly
  • Change oil and filter every 5,000–10,000 km
  • Pay attention to dashboard warning lights
